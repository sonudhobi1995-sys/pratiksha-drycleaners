About Us

Pratiksha Dry Cleaners was founded in 1987. It is not just a shop; it is the result of the immense hard work and honesty of my parents. For over 39 years, we have served the beautiful community of Ambabari, Jaipur with dedication.

From the streets of Durga Park to the heart of Hanuman Market, we have earned the trust of families who treat us like their own. We believe that every garment carries a memory, and we handle those memories with the utmost care.

"The heat of our steam irons never burns the cloth; it only strengthens the trust we have built over decades of struggle and service."

Today, guided by the principles of our "Kul-Deepak Mahasanhita", we combine traditional values with modern technology to ensure your clothes stay as fresh as your trust in us.
