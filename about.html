Our Journey

The foundation of Pratiksha Dry Cleaners was laid in 1987 in the heart of Ambabari, Jaipur. More than just a business, this institution is the living embodiment of the immense hard work, integrity, and relentless struggle of my parents.

Our journey began as 'Bhanwarlal Laundry,' a time when our greatest assets were not machines, but a strong will and honest labor. For nearly four decades, from the lanes of Durga Park to the heart of Hanuman Market, we have served families who have embraced us as their own.

"The warmth of my father’s hands and the unwavering patience of my mother still live within the steam that smooths every wrinkle today. We haven't just cleaned clothes; we have earned trust."

Today, as we move forward, we have merged our traditional roots with state-of-the-art technology to transform into 'Pratiksha Dry Cleaners'—a brand built on legacy but driven by innovation.
