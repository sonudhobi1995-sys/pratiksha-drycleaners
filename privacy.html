Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pratiksha Dry Cleaners. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it to serve you better at our Ambabari store.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you book a service, such as your name, phone number, and residential address for pickup and delivery services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for business purposes, including processing your orders, sending pickup/delivery updates via WhatsApp, and improving our customer service quality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties. Your information is only shared with our internal team members who are directly involved in your garment care and delivery.

5. Security of Your Data

We implement professional security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Digital records are protected, and physical logs are kept secure within our premises.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you or ask for its deletion from our records at any time by contacting us directly.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out to Sonu Bhai at +91 77408 70095 or visit us at Hanuman Market, Ambabari, Jaipur.
