Our 7-Star Fabric Care Philosophy

Premium Care

Heritage Dry Cleaning

Since 1987, we have mastered the art of cleaning without damage. We use eco-friendly, fabric-safe solvents that penetrate deep into the fibers to remove oils and stains while preserving the garment's original luster. Ideal for Silk, Designer Wear, and sensitive fabrics.

Winter Protection

Specialized Woolen Care

Woolens require a gentle touch. Our process involves a cold-soak purification method and controlled air-drying to prevent pilling, stretching, or shrinkage.

Avoid the 'shine marks' of traditional irons. Our industrial high-pressure steam tables sanitize and relax fabric fibers, providing a crisp finish.
