Our 7-Star Fabric Care Philosophy

Premium Care

Heritage Dry Cleaning

Since 1987, we have mastered the art of cleaning without damage. We use eco-friendly, fabric-safe solvents that penetrate deep into the fibers to remove oils and stains while preserving the garment's original luster. Ideal for Silk, Designer Wear, and sensitive fabrics.

Winter Protection

Specialized Woolen Care

Woolens require a gentle touch. Our process involves a cold-soak purification method and controlled air-drying to prevent pilling, stretching, or shrinkage. We treat your Pashminas and coats with the warmth they deserve.

Advanced Finishing

Professional Steam Ironing

Avoid the 'shine marks' of traditional irons. Our industrial high-pressure steam tables sanitize and relax fabric fibers, providing a crisp, boutique-like finish. Your formals and ethnic wear will look sharp and feel fresh.

Preserving Memories

Wedding & Couture Care

A wedding outfit is an emotion. We provide manual 'spot-cleaning' for heavy Lehengas and Sherwanis, ensuring every bead and zari is protected. Each piece is tissue-wrapped to prevent oxidation and long-term fiber damage.

Deep Disinfection

Home Linen & Decor

Remove dust mites and allergens from your heavy curtains, bedspreads, and sofa covers. Our deep-cleaning process ensures your home environment stays healthy and vibrant, without any distortion in the shape of large items.

Revival Service

Leather & Shoe Spa

Leather needs hydration. We use premium conditioning oils to restore the suppleness of your leather jackets and suede shoes. We revive the natural texture and provide a protective layer against dust and moisture.

Fast Track

Same-Day / Next-Day Service

Need it quick? Our professional team operates from 09:00 AM to 09:00 PM to ensure your urgent orders are priority-tracked. We guarantee the fastest possible turnaround within our working hours for your rapid needs.
